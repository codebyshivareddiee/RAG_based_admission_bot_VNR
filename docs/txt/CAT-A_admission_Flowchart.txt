VNR Vignana Jyothi Institute of Engineering and Technology
Admission Section

Category A Reporting Flowchart – Admissions 2025

Overview:
Students admitted under the Convenor Quota (Category A) must complete the following reporting steps in their respective departments.

------------------------------------------------------------
DOCUMENTS REQUIRED FOR CATEGORY A (CONVENOR QUOTA) ADMISSION
------------------------------------------------------------

Students must bring the following documents for admission:

MANDATORY DOCUMENTS:
1. TS EAMCET / JEE Main Rank Card (original + 2 photocopies)
2. Intermediate (10+2) Marks Memo (original + 2 photocopies)
3. SSC (10th Class) Marks Memo (original + 2 photocopies)
4. Transfer Certificate (TC) - Original
5. Migration Certificate (if applicable)
6. Aadhar Card (photocopy)
7. Recent passport-size photographs (6 copies)
8. Study Certificates from Class 6 to Intermediate
9. Local / Non-local Status Certificate
10. Bonafide Certificate from previous institution

FOR RESERVED CATEGORIES:
11. Caste Certificate (for SC/ST/BC/EWS categories)
12. Income Certificate (for fee reimbursement eligibility)

ADDITIONAL DOCUMENTS (if applicable):
13. Sports Certificate (if claiming sports quota)
14. Physical Disability Certificate (if applicable)
15. NCC Certificate (if applicable)

Important Notes:
- All original certificates will be verified and returned after admission
- Keep 2 photocopies of each document
- Ensure all documents are arranged in the order mentioned above
- Missing documents may delay the admission process

------------------------------------------------------------
STEP 1: Scanning Documents & Submitting Google Form
------------------------------------------------------------

1. Identify student volunteers to assist parents and students.
2. Arrange Wi-Fi for internet connectivity in the reporting room.
3. Ask students to download the Adobe Scanner app to scan certificates.
4. Ensure:
   - Each document is scanned individually.
   - Each file is properly renamed.
5. Provide QR code of the Google Form and ask students to:
   - Scan the QR code.
   - Fill in all required details.
   - Upload certificates in the respective fields.
6. Ask students to check their email for acknowledgement after submission.
7. Verify acknowledgement email.
8. Issue:
   - Undertaking Letter
   - Student Basic Profile form
9. Instruct students to arrange physical documents as per checklist order for submission.

------------------------------------------------------------
STEP 2: Eduprime Reporting
------------------------------------------------------------

Portal Navigation:
Admissions → Allotment → Enter Hall Ticket Number

Process:
1. Enter hall ticket number.
2. Fill required particulars displayed on screen.
3. Verify details and click Submit.
   - Do NOT print Allotment Order.
4. Go to:
   Certificates → Enter Hall Ticket Number
5. Verify required documents.
6. Check relevant boxes.
7. Enter remarks clearly.
8. Click Submit.
9. Print acknowledgement copies (3 pages will be generated).

Printed Pages:
- Page 1:
  File copy.
  Must be signed by student and parent.
  Place with certificate file.

- Page 2:
  Signed copy to be given to student (ONLY after fee payment).
  Hand over during Step 4.

- Page 3:
  Transport/Hostel acknowledgement.
  Optional for parents but must be provided.

Important Note:
Page 2 should be handed over only after fee payment along with the temporary ID card.
For discrepancies, contact Admission Section.

------------------------------------------------------------
STEP 3: Fee Payment
------------------------------------------------------------

Fees must be paid through:
- Google Pay (GPay)
- PhonePe
- Payment link sent to registered mobile number

------------------------------------------------------------
STEP 4: Collecting Acknowledgement Receipt & Temporary ID Card
------------------------------------------------------------

1. Verify fee payment receipt.
2. Hand over:
   - Acknowledgement receipt (Page 2 from Step 2).
3. Issue temporary ID card.

Temporary ID Instructions:
- Student must stick photograph on printed name card.
- Faculty must stamp across photo and card.
- Student must wear ID until permanent card is issued.

Additional Notes:
- Temporary ID cards are issued by the Admission Section.
- Collect them from the section on the same day.
- Tags and pouches may be collected in advance.
