APPLICATION FEES

B.Tech Application Fee : ₹2,000
M.Tech Application Fee : ₹1,000

⚠️ Any fee paid is non-refundable.

**UNDERGRADUATE PROGRAMMES (UG)**

**Admission to First Year – 4-Year B.Tech Programme**

B.Tech Branch Code (EAPCET): **VJEC**

Admissions into undergraduate programmes (B.Tech) for all branches are made as per the relevant Rules of the Government and norms/guidelines of the Telangana Council of Higher Education.

Seat Distribution:

70% of seats – Category-A (Convenor Quota)

Based on rank obtained in state level entrance examination – TGEAPCET.

Seats will be filled by the Convener, TGEAPCET.

30% of seats – Management Quota

Management includes:
- Category-B
- NRI

Filled by the management as per the relevant rules of the State Government.

As per guidelines issued by the Telangana Council of Higher Education from time to time.

Spot Admission Procedure for B.Tech

The Convenor office, TGEAPCET will release notification for filling vacancy seats.

The College issues an advertisement after notification release.

The institute fills seats as per the guidelines issued by the Convener, TGEAPCET.

Admission under FN / OCI / CIWG (Supernumerary Seats)

As per GO.Ms.No:49 and circulars issued by AICTE:

Supernumerary seats are additional seats (over and above sanctioned intake) filled under:

Foreign Nationals (FN)

Overseas Citizenship of India (OCI)

Children of Indian Workers in Gulf Countries (CIWG)

All admissions under this category are subject to approval of competent authorities.

Note: Supernumerary quota applies to B.Tech ONLY. It is NOT applicable for M.Tech / MCA.

Lateral Entry – Admission to Second Year B.Tech

Up to 10% of the sanctioned intake of each programme shall be filled by the Convener, TGECET.

Based on merit ranks obtained in TGECET (State Level Common Entrance Test).

Spot Admission Procedure for B.Tech Lateral Entry:

Admission will be made based on the guidelines issued by the Convener, TGECET.

The College issues an advertisement after necessary approval.

Spot admissions will be made according to the guidelines issued from time to time by the Convener, TGECET.

All admissions made at institute level (Spot Admissions) are subject to approval of competent authorities.

================================
**POST GRADUATE ADMISSIONS (PG)**

Admissions into Postgraduate programmes (M.Tech & MCA) are made as per the rules of the Telangana Council of Higher Education (TGCHE).

M.Tech / MCA Branch Code (TGPGECET/TGICET): **VJEC1**

Fee Structure (2025):
- M.Tech Fee: ₹1,30,000 per annum (applicable for academic year 2025)
- MCA Fee: ₹27,000 per annum (applicable for academic year 2025)
- ⚠️ Any fee paid is non-refundable.

Admission to M.Tech & MCA – Two Year Post Graduate Programme

70% seats – Category-A

For M.Tech: Based on TGPGECET and GATE ranks.

For MCA: Based on TGICET ranks.

Seats will be filled by the Convener.

30% seats – Management

Management includes:
- Category-B
- NRI

Filled by the management as per Government rules and TGCHE guidelines.

(Note: Supernumerary quota is NOT applicable for M.Tech / MCA admissions.)

All admissions made at institute level (Spot Admissions and Management admissions) are subject to approval of competent authorities.

Spot Admission Procedure for M.Tech & MCA

The Convenor office, TGPGECET & TGICET will release notification for filling vacancy seats.

The College issues an advertisement after notification release.

The institute will fill seats as per the guidelines issued by the Convener, TGPGECET & TGICET.
