------------------------------------------------------------
SECTION 6: STUDENT ELIGIBILITY FOR CAMPUS PLACEMENT
------------------------------------------------------------

Mandatory Conditions:

- Minimum 75% attendance in training classes.
- Registration in 3rd year (2nd semester).
- Submission of latest CV (online).
- Submission of 5 passport size photographs.
- Submission of required certificates (soft and hard copies).
- Follow dress code norms.

Placement Rules:

- Maximum 5 attempts in campus drives.
- Once selected, student cannot attend further drives (except Dream Company).
- After Dream Company selection, no further drives allowed.
- If absent after confirming interview, removed from eligible list.
