FOREIGN ADMISSIONS
------------------------------------------------------------

Supernumerary Quota Seats are available for:
- Foreign Nationals (FN)
- Overseas Citizens of India (OCI)
- Children of Indian Workers in Gulf Countries (CIWG)

Eligibility Criteria:

Foreign Nationals:
- Student must be a citizen of another country.
- Must have completed education abroad.
Proof:
- Passport and Visa documents of the student.

OCI:
- Student must be an OCI cardholder.
Proof:
- Passport and OCI card of the student.

CIWG:
- Parent must be working in GCC countries with valid Visa/Work Permit.
Proof:
- Visa/Work Permit/Residence Permit of parent valid during admission year.
