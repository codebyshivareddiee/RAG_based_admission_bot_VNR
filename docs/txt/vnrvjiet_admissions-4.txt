About VNRVJIET
--------------
VNR Vignana Jyothi Institute of Engineering and Technology (VNRVJIET) is a premier autonomous engineering college located in Bachupally, Hyderabad, Telangana, India. Established in 1995, the college is affiliated to Jawaharlal Nehru Technological University, Hyderabad (JNTUH) and approved by AICTE, New Delhi. The institute was granted autonomous status by UGC in 2009. VNRVJIET is accredited by NAAC with an 'A++' grade and multiple programs are accredited by NBA.

Admission Process – B.Tech
--------------------------
Admissions to VNRVJIET are conducted through the following modes:

B.Tech Branch Code (EAPCET): **VJEC**

1. **Convenor / Category-A** – 70% of seats
   - Through Telangana State Engineering, Agriculture, Pharmacy and Common Entrance Test (TS EAPCET)
   - Admissions handled by Telangana State Council of Higher Education (TSCHE)
   - Candidates must register on the TS EAPCET counselling portal and select VNRVJIET during web counselling

2. **Management – Category-B / NRI / NRI Sponsored** – 30% of seats
   - Through college-level counselling
   - Based on TS EAPCET / JEE Main rank
   - Includes NRI and NRI Sponsored candidates
   - Apply directly at the VNRVJIET admissions office or website

3. **Supernumerary** (FN / OCI / CIWG) – Additional Seats (over and above sanctioned intake)
   - For Foreign Nationals (FN), Overseas Citizens of India (OCI), and Children of Indian Workers in Gulf Countries (CIWG)
   - Eligibility: OCI cardholders, OR children of parents working in GCC countries (Bahrain, Kuwait, Oman, Qatar, Saudi Arabia, UAE) with a valid Visa/Work Permit, OR Foreign Nationals
   - Fee (2025-26): $5000/yr (CSE/ECE/IT/AI branches), $3500/yr (Civil/EEE/ME), $3000/yr (Auto/EIE)
   - Overall NRI/FN/OCI quota fee structure is $3,000–$5,000, may vary according to branch
   - Contact admissions office for step-by-step process: admissionsenquiry@vnrvjiet.in | +91-40-2304 2758
   - ⚠️ VNRVJIET does NOT use agents/consultants for NRI/Category-B admissions

Admission Process – M.Tech / MCA
---------------------------------
M.Tech / MCA Branch Code (TGPGECET/TGICET): **VJEC1**

Admission Quotas (M.Tech / MCA):
1. **Convenor / Category-A** – 70% of seats (TGPGECET / GATE for M.Tech; TGICET for MCA)
2. **Management – Category-B / NRI / NRI Sponsored** – 30% of seats

(Note: Supernumerary quota is NOT applicable for M.Tech / MCA admissions.)

Eligibility Criteria
--------------------
- Must have passed Intermediate (10+2) or equivalent with Mathematics, Physics, and Chemistry (MPC)
- Minimum 45% aggregate in Intermediate (40% for reserved categories)
- Valid TS EAPCET rank or JEE Main score
- Telangana state domicile required for Convenor Quota

Frequently Asked Questions
--------------------------
Q: Is VNRVJIET a good college?
A: VNRVJIET is one of the top private engineering colleges in Telangana, accredited with NAAC A++ grade and NBA accreditation for multiple programs.

Q: What is the TS EAPCET code for VNRVJIET?
A: The B.Tech Branch Code for VNRVJIET in EAPCET counselling is **VJEC**. The M.Tech / MCA Branch Code is **VJEC1**.

Q: Is hostel mandatory?
A: No, hostel is optional. Outstation students are encouraged to opt for hostel.

Q: Does VNRVJIET accept JEE Main scores?
A: Yes, for Management Quota admissions, JEE Main scores are accepted.

Q: What is the nearest metro station?
A: JNTU Metro Station on the Green Line is approximately 5 km from the campus.
