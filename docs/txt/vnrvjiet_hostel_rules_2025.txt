Source: Official Hostel Rules Document

------------------------------------------------------------
1. STUDENT INFORMATION REQUIREMENTS
------------------------------------------------------------

- Students must provide correct parent/guardian address and contact details.
- Any change in permanent address must be informed immediately.
- Hostel ID card must be carried at all times and produced when required.

------------------------------------------------------------
2. DISCIPLINE & CONDUCT
------------------------------------------------------------

- Maintain cleanliness of rooms and hostel premises.
- Loud noise and disturbances are strictly prohibited.
- Posters or captions on walls/doors are not allowed.
- No staying in rooms during college hours without Warden permission.
- Unauthorized room exchange is not allowed.
- Celebrations or parties (including birthdays) are strictly prohibited.
- No outdoor games inside hostel premises.
- Principal/Warden decision is final in all hostel matters.
- Management may frame, amend or revoke hostel rules at any time.

------------------------------------------------------------
3. FEES & ACCOMMODATION
------------------------------------------------------------

- Hostel fee must be paid before the academic year begins.
- Renewal of hostel accommodation depends on semester performance.
- Fees and charges are subject to revision by management.
- Room must be handed over personally to Warden/Supervisor at year end.

------------------------------------------------------------
4. ANTI-RAGGING & PROHIBITED ACTIVITIES
------------------------------------------------------------

- Ragging is a criminal offence and strictly prohibited.
- Possession or use of intoxicants is prohibited:
  - Alcohol
  - Drugs
  - Gutka
  - Tobacco
  - Cigarettes
  - Sedative materials
- Outside food is not allowed.
- No intentional wastage of food.
- Must follow mess timings.
- Two-wheelers and four-wheelers are not allowed inside hostel.

------------------------------------------------------------
5. VISITORS & LEAVE POLICY
------------------------------------------------------------

- Guests are not allowed in rooms without prior Warden permission.
- Parents/local guardians allowed only on Sundays and public holidays.
- Visiting hours: 4:00 PM to 6:00 PM.
- Students leaving the station must:
  - Apply for leave two days in advance.
  - Obtain written permission.
  - Provide address of stay during leave period.

------------------------------------------------------------
6. SECURITY & INSPECTION
------------------------------------------------------------

- Hostel authorities are not responsible for personal item loss 
  (mobile phones, laptops, gadgets).
- Management has authority to inspect:
  - Rooms
  - Bags
  - Almirahs
  - Personal belongings
- Management not responsible for accidents inside/outside campus.

------------------------------------------------------------
7. DAMAGE & PENALTIES
------------------------------------------------------------

- Students must not damage hostel property.
- If damage is identified:
  - Responsible students will pay for the loss.
- If responsible students cannot be identified:
  - Collective fine will be imposed.
- Objectionable incidents may lead to:
  - Penalty
  - Expulsion from hostel
