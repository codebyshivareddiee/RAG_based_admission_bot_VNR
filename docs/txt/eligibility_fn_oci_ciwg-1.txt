 Eligibility Criteria for FN/OCI/CIWG Category (Supernumerary Quota – B.Tech Only)

Note: Supernumerary / FN / OCI / CIWG quota is applicable for B.Tech admissions ONLY.
It is NOT available for M.Tech or MCA admissions.

Application Fee: ₹2,000 (B.Tech) | ₹1,000 (M.Tech)
⚠️ Any fee paid is non-refundable.

Admissions under the Foreign category are open to candidates who meet any of the following eligibility conditions:

OCI Card Holders:
The student is an OCI cardholder.
Proof: Passport and OCI card of the student.

CIWG Category:
The student must have a parent who works in GCC countries with a valid Visa or Work permit.
Proof: The parents' visa/work permit/residence permit with a validity date applicable to the admissions year.
(The Gulf Cooperation Council (GCC) includes Bahrain, Kuwait, Oman, Qatar, Saudi Arabia, and the United Arab Emirates.)

Foreign Nationals:
The student is a citizen of another country and has completed their education abroad.
Proof: Passport, educational records, and visa documents of the student and the passport of the parents.
